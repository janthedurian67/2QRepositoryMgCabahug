    Game 1: Forsaken

  • FORSAKEN

    • Short Description:

    Forsaken is a horror themed game where you

    can play as one of 2 roles. You can either play

    as a survivor or as the killer. As a survivor,

    You are given abilities for the character that you

    chose during intermission time.

    You must work with your team and cooperate

    with each other to play time against the killer

    so that they run out of time and the game is over.

    As the killer, you must try to eliminate all

    the survivors before time runs out. You can do

    achieve that by using the abilities that killers

    have to help you find and eliminate survivors.
